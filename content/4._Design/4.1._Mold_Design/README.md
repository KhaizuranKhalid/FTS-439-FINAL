As previously mentioned, the current mould will be reused to manufacture the new rims.
To create this mould, the rim was 3D scanned with a Creaform Handyscan with the help of RVI
Inspection and Equipment. This 3D scan was then sent to Admiralty International to create a 
plug for the mould to be formed up. From experience, the mould has a good surface finish and
good seating surface for the tire bead to seal against. More importantly, the mould has 
adequate area surrounding the rim for the VARTM bagging and expendable materials. The red area
highlighted below shows a small inconsistency but does not affect the design.
