Although the previous jig could be reused, the sub-optimal mounting did not instill confidence.
The new jig has improved
retention thanks to the addition of clamps that is mounted flush to the wheel to 
prevent side to side play. The new jig is heightened to allow for the tires to be mounted for
the suspension department. This allows for the flexibility to conduct tests with a 
tire mounted should the need arise. Finally, the new jig also allows to mount the original Keizer
centre piece that would allow for flexibility in our loading conditions. However, the original steel
plate that was used to mount the carbon fiber rims will need to modified in order to mate to the new
shaft. The applicator was designed with a spread angle that was projected by the contact patch of the tire to
the centre of the wheel.
