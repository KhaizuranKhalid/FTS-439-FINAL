## **Market Options**
The easiest way to implement carbon fiber wheels is to purchase them from existing manufacturers such as OZ Racing and Rotobox. However, these wheels are often very expensive starting at `$`3,000 and require the hub a redesign to fit the wheel. This redesign would lock the car to the new wheels, preventing the use of old wheels as testing sets. Finally, the majority of these wheels use a one-piece design which makes it difficult to repair in case of damage but more importantly, difficult to fit tires in-house or with automotive workshops in Singapore due to the lack of experience with carbon fiber wheels.

## **Prepreg VS Dry Fabric**
Prepreg carbon fiber is a very high performing material but it comes at a cost. In the previous iteration, amount of carbon fiber came in at over `$`6,000 for a set of wheels. A similar performing material is dry carbon fiber fabric which is significantly cheaper but requires the use of epoxy resin to bind the fibers together. Additionally, prepreg carbon fiber requires a freezer for storage and a specialised oven for curing, increasing the overhead cost of using prepreg carbon fiber. For the same area, the cost of dry fabric is under `$`4,000.
