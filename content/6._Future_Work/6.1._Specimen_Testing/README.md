The bulk of these properties can be achieved with the following tests:

1. **ASTM D3039**
<div></div>
> Tensile Strength

2. **ASTM D3410**
<div></div>
> Compressive strength

3. **ASTM D3518**
<div></div>
> In-plane shear strength

4. **ASTM D7264**
<div></div>
> Flexural strength and stiffness
