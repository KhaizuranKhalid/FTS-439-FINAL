To optimise the layups, areas that are deemed high 
stress can be thickened while the lower stress areas could be thinned out. Also, dry fabrics are easier to conform as compared
to stiffer prepreg fabric. The shape of the plies could be optimised to cut away low stress areas to further reduce material
and thus mass. The example below shows a rim with just 3 plies, base, side and edge.
