Before the start of design phase, a meeting was held to discuss on the shortcomings
of the outgoing car. This allows us to highlight manufacturing, reliability, maintenance and 
performance deficiencies. Collectively, the team will agree on a goal to achieve
for the next car.


## **Team Goal**
Decrease average lap times in endurance by 10% through improved reliability.

## **Department Goal**
Design, optimise and implement composite rims to reduce wheel mass by 10% 
while reducing cost from previous implementation.
