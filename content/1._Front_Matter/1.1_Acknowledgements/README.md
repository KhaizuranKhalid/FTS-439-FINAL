I would like to express my gratitude to emeritus professor Seah Kar Heng, supervisors Mr Lim Hong Wee and Mr Kenneth Lim for their guidance and support throughout this project. I am especially grateful to emeritus professor Seah Kar Heng for inspiring us and equipping our team with the basic theoretical knowledge of race car design to get us started with our own works. His passion for automotive engineering and his willingness to share his expertise have been crucial in building our foundational understanding of the principles that drive our work. My sincere appreciation also goes to supervisors Mr Lim Hong Wee and Mr Kenneth Lim for their mentorship and continuous support. Their experience and wisdom guided us through the challenges of concept generation and the design process. The practical insights and thoughtful feedback they offered have been invaluable in helping me and the team realise our ideas into a functional, well-designed car. Especially to Mr Kenneth Lim for his experience and expertise in CFRP that allowed me to consult extreme ideas for this project.

I would like to thank our sponsors for not only providing funding for our project, but also for technical consultations that allowed us to better understand the engineering principles to build the best car we could. Their passion and assistance in our journey proved to us there was always room for passion in the working life.

I would also like to thank our FSAE alumni, whose mentorship was especially important during the beginning of our journey. Their willingness to share their experiences and guide us through the early stages of the R25e season helped bridge the gap between theoretical understanding and hands-on application. Without their experience, we would have been lost trying to design out own parts without understanding the contexts of the designs. Their feedback and advice across various design areas, along with their lessons in manufacturing and design, played a crucial role in shaping our project’s outcome.

To my teammates, I am deeply appreciative of the compassion and collaboration that everyone has brought to this project. With everyone contributing through distinct roles and responsibilities, we will collectively created something remarkable. Building a race car within a single year tested our teamwork, patience, and problem-solving skills, but it also brought out the best in all of us. I am proud of what we have achieved together and the friendships we have forged.

Finally, I would like to thank my family for their patience, understanding, and constant encouragement. It was not easy for them to miss out time together due in part to the late nights we stayed up to prepare the car for our tests. Supporting me through this allowed me to give my best to the team and our project.

This journey has been both challenging and deeply rewarding. The knowledge, mentorship, and teamwork I have experienced throughout this process have shaped not only the success of the project but also my own growth as an engineer. I look forward to the next competition for us to showcase our efforts and strive to be better than the previous years.
